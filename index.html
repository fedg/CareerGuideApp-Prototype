GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Designers work with clients developing creative and innovative solution for brand and company needs. Working in print, web, and other mediums designers focus on connecting with their audience in a way that properly portrays their client's values.

    How to get into the field

    The graphic design industry is about constant collaboration and connection. As such, making valuable connections and building professional relationships is important for breaking in to the field. A strong portfolio of work tailored to the area of work you wish to break into is the main vessel by which you will display your work talent, along with interviewing and conversational skills.

    What to expect on the job

    In doing design work, regardless of the respective sector you are working within, you will be interacting and involved with clients on various projects. Most work is done in teams and will involve collaborating and co-ordinating with 2 or more individuals in order to complete projects from concept to delivery. Dealing with printers, productions companies as well as for-hire free lancers is also a large part of the daily work in the graphic design industry.

    How to be successful in this career

    What it really comes down to with graphic design in terms of success is openess to input, suggetions and collaboration. As most work is performed in teams, strong team work and collaboration skills are key, so as to produce the highest quality projects that plays on the strengths of each team member, creating a synergy of skills and strengths.
